### Compiler Optimizations (Planned)

See `specs/optimizations.md` for full catalog with effort/impact/dependency details.

- ‚úÖ **Borrow inference and elision (P7)** ‚Äî `lib/tir/borrow.ml` + `lib/tir/perceus.ml` integration. Pre-Perceus analysis marks TCon/TString/TPtr params as borrowed when they are only read (pattern-matched, field-accessed) and never stored, returned, or passed to owning positions. Fixpoint inter-procedural: params passed only to other borrowed positions remain borrowed. RC elision: (a) call sites skip EIncRC for borrowed args still live after the call; (b) callee suppresses scrutinee EDecRC for borrowed params; (c) last-use borrowed args get caller-side EDecRC instead of callee-side. Eliminates 100% of RC overhead for read-only middlewares (HTTP Conn pattern). 10 new tests in `borrow_inference` group.
